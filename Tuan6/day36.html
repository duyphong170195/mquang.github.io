brave /breɪv/ (adj) dũng cảm, gan dạ
synonym: (adj) courageous
  • It was a brave decision to quit her job and start her own business.
  • He was brave enough to go there alone.
  • He is brave enough to go there by himself.
  • Tom is one of the bravest men I've ever known.
  • How brave of him to jump into the water to save the little girl!
  • (n) bravery: sự dũng cảm, lòng dũng cảm
  • They were awarded medals for their bravery.
  • (n) bravado: ra vẻ can đảm
    - a show of bravery, especially when unnecessary and dangerous, to make people admire you
    - pretended courage or defiant confidence when one is really afraid
  • “Don't threaten me,” she whispered with false bravado.

    courage /ˈkə.rɪdʒ/ (n) sự dũng cảm, gan dạ
    synonym: (n) bravery
  • It took me several months to get up the courage to ask her to lunch.
  • After years of abuse from her husband, she eventually found the courage to leave him.
  • (adj) courageous: can đảm, dũng cảm
  • I hope people will be courageous enough to speak out against this injustice.
  • The courageous firefighters rushed into the burning building to save the trapped women and children.

    inspire /ɪnˈspaɪ.ər/ (v) truyền cảm hứng

  • After her trip to Venezuela, she felt inspired to learn Spanish.
  • What an inspiring speaker!

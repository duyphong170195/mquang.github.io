excellent /ˈek.sə.lənt/ (adj) xuất sắc, ưu tú, tuyệt vời
  • The food was excellent.
  • Tom is an excellent lawyer.
  • The restaurant is well known for its friendly atmosphere and excellent service.
  • He has excellent despite his age.
  • She is an excellent .
  • (n) excellence: sự xuất sắc, ưu tú, trội
  • This college has a long tradition of athletic excellence.
  • His excellence in baseball earned him a scholarship
  • (v) excel + in/at: trội về, xuất sắc về (môn gì)
  • She has always excelled in foreign languages.
  • As a child he excelled at music and art.
  • I have nothing I feel I can excel at.

    quantity /ˈkwɑn.tə.t̮i/ (n) số lượng
  • The police found a quantity of drugs at his home.
  • We consumed vast quantities of food and drink that night.
  • A large quantity of beer was sold.

  • It's difficult to quantify how many people will be affected by the change.

    quality /ˈkwɑ.lə.t̮i/ (n)
    1. chất lượng
    2. đức tính, phẩm chất
  • I only buy good-quality wine.
  • The quality of the picture on our television isn't very good.
  • I don't think he has the right qualities to be a teacher.
  • We’re looking for someone who loves children and has the qualities of a good teacher.
  • (v) qualify: đủ điều kiện, đủ tư cách, đủ khả năng, đủ tiêu chuẩn (FINISH TRAINING)
  • This training course will qualify you for a better job.
  • He qualified as a vet last year.
  • This course qualifies you to teach in any secondary school.
  • She hopes to qualify (as a lawyer) at the end of the year.
  • It takes three years to qualify as a social worker.

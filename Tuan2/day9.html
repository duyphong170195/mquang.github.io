Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
momentary /ˈmoʊ.mən.te.ri/ (adj) chốc lát, một thoáng (khoảng thời gian rất ngắn)
  • A momentary look of anxiety passed across his face.
  • There was a momentary pause.
  • There was a momentary hesitation before he replied.
  • After a momentary silence, the President spoke again.
    		•
    temporary /ˈtem.pə.re.ri/ (adj) tạm thời
  • temporary accommodation.
  • temporary tatto
  • I'm looking for some temporary work.
  • The supermarket is taking on a few temporary assistants for the Christmas season.
  • (adj) permanent: dài hạn, lâu dài, cố định (antonym)
  • Are you looking for a temporary or a permanent job?
  • She is looking for a permanent place to stay.
    		•
    temperature /ˈtem.prə.tʃər/ (n) nhiệt độ
  • The temperature today reached 25°C.
  • to take someone's temperature: đo, lấy nhiệt độ của ai
  • The doctor examined him and took his temperature.
  • to have (run) a temperature: lên cơn sốt
  • Does he have a temperature (= is it higher than normal, because of illness)
  • She's running a temperature (= it is higher than normal).
    		•
    promotion /prəˈmoʊ.ʃn/ (n) sự thăng cấp, sự thăng chức
  • I want a job with good prospects for promotion.
  • I got a promotion today.
  • By working hard he gained rapid promotion.
  • You get an automatic promotion after two years.
  • Staff members eligible for promotion be notified by management within ten days.
  • (v) promote
    		•
    promote /prəˈmoʊt/ (v)
    1. thúc đẩy, đẩy mạnh
    2. thăng chức, thăng cấp
    3. quảng cáo, quảng bá
  • The government started a program to promote the industry.
  • The government is promoting the use of domestically made products.
  • He has been promoted to sergeant.
  • She has been promoted twice since she joined this company.
  • The band are currently touring to promote their new album.
  • The area is being promoted as a tourist destination.
    		•
    procedure /prəˈsiːdʒər/ (n)
    1. thủ tục, quy trình


    - a series of steps followed in a regular definite order
    2. (y học) ca phẫu thuật, quy trình phẫu thuật, quá trình phẫu thuật (a operation)
    3. (máy tính) chương trình con (another name for subroutine)
    Để ý ta thấy 3 nghĩa này đều mang một nghĩa chung là một chuỗi hành động, công việc cần thực hiện
    		 1.
  • What's the procedure for applying for a visa?
  • The company has new procedures for dealing with complaints.
  • Don't worry - I'll go through the procedure with you step by step.
  • Is this the procedure?
  • An interview is an important part of our selection procedure.
    2.
  • It's a routine/standard surgical procedure.
  • the procedure for treating a burn.
  • It’s a difficult procedure that can take 7 or 8 hours.
  • Liposuction is a minor surgical procedure.

    • 3.
    Phân biệt chương trình con (procedure) và hàm (function):
    + Procedure là chuỗi tập hợp các dòng lệnh (command) thực hiện nhiệm vụ nhưng không cần trả về (return) giá trị nào
    từ khóa (kiểu dữ liệu) void cho phép ta tạo (khai báo) các phương thức mà không cần trả về giá trị nào
    + Function khác Procedure là nó có trả về giá trị

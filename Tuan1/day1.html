belongings /bɪˈlɔːŋ.ɪŋz/ (n) đồ đạc
  • I put a few personal belongings in a bag and left the house for the last time.
  • Passengers are reminded to take all their personal belongings with them when they leave the plane.
  • Do you really need to bring so many of your belongings with you on this trip?

    luggage /ˈlʌ.ɡɪdʒ/ (n) hành lý
  • We dropped our luggage off at the hotel and went sightseeing.
  • You stay there with the luggage while I find a cab.
  • If your luggage is overweight, you'll have to pay extra.
    		•
    merchandise /ˈmɜːr.tʃən.daɪs/ (n) hàng hóa (để mua/bán)
  • Japan exported $117 billion in merchandise to the US in 1999.
  • Please do not touch the merchandise.
  • No merchandise can be returned.
    		•
    mayor US: /ˈmeɪ.ər/
    UK: /meə/    		 (n) thị trưởng
  • He is the mayor of Beijing.
  • My uncle was the mayor of Memphis.
  • We need a mayor who is tough enough to clean up this town.
    		•
    major /ˈmeɪ.dʒər/ (adj) chính, chủ yếu, phần lớn
    (n)
    1. chuyên ngành
    2. thiếu tá (quân đội)
  • Citrus fruits are a major source of vitamin C.
  • Tokyo and New York are major financial centres.
  • What is your major, English or French?
  • She was a philosophy major at an Ivy League college.
  • Her father was a major in the Scots Guards.
  • (n) majority: phần lớn, đa số
  • The majority of the employees have university degrees.
  • What do you spend a majority of your time doing?
  • Tom spends a majority of his time helping Mary.
  • She spends a majority of her time taking care of her children.
    		•
    manner /ˈmæ.nər/ (n)
  • cách thức
  • lối, thói, kiểu
    		•  table manners (phép tắc ăn uống, phép lịch sự tại bàn ăn)
    satisfied /ˈsæ.t̬ɪs.faɪd/ (adj) hài lòng, vừa ý, thỏa mãn
  • Are you satisfied with the new arrangement?
  • We are not satisfied with these results.
  • Some people are never satisfied!
  • (n) satisfaction: sự (cảm giác) vừa lòng, thỏa mãn, toại nguyện
  • (adj) satisfactory: làm thỏa mãn, làm vừa ý, đạt yêu cầu => dùng cho vật
    • + satisfied dùng cho người. Ví dụ: Bạn làm một bài văn nộp cho cô giáo. Cô giáo hài lòng với bài làm của bạn:
    + The teacher is satisfied with your essay.
    Bài văn của bạn làm hài lòng cô giáo:
    + The essay is satisfactory.
    examine /ɪɡˈzæ.mɪn/ (v) xem xét, khảo sát, nghiên cứu
  • We were examined on European history.
  • You'll be examined in three main areas; speaking, listening, and reading comprehension.
  • X-rays are used by doctors to examine the bones or organs inside our body.
  • We need to examine how an accident like this can be avoided in the future.
  • (n) exam (viết tắt của examination): sự thi cử, khảo sát, kì thi
